AVer Video Conferencing Enhances Business Communication

AVer provided us with a great turn-key solution to our Video Conferencing needs. 'quality' and 'ease of use'.

VC320

AVer's VC320 Conference Camera offers high-quality video conferencing superior to purely software-based systems, but without the expense of traditional hardware. Extremely easy to use, the VC320 needs only be plugged into a computer via its plug-and-play USB connection for users to enjoy crystal-clear video and audio with nearly any cloud- or web-based video conferencing platform. Moreover, the VC320 boasts a compact design combining a microphone, speaker and electronic Pan Tilt Zoom camera in a single easy-to-carry device, making it the perfect mobile video collaboration solution.


Compatible Applications

EVC130P

The EVC130P 1080p Video Conferencing End Point with 16x PTZ Camera is the ultimate large group video conferencing solution. Perfect for groups of 15+, the 1080p HD resolution and wide-angle PTZ camera with 16X optical zoom lets you see and interact with everyone while quickly zeroing in for close-ups.

EVC300

The EVC300 is an embedded 4-site HD MCU with built-in 16x PTZ Video Conferencing Endpoint - perfect for large rooms in main offices or auditoriums. The EVC300 allows you to connect with up to 4 parties, all in HD. Integrate the EVC300 with other VC systems, mobile and/or desktop solutions without cloud service subscriptions. With a multitude of IO ports, the EVC300 is the ideal solution for room integration projects, as well.

EVC900

With a multitude of IO ports, the EVC900 is the ideal solution for room integration projects, as well.
